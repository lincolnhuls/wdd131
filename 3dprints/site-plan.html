Overview

Purpose

This site will act as an online catalog of all the items that are currently in stock. This site will provide the information a user needs to inspect the stock and contact those needed to make a purchase.

Audience

The audience of this website will be those that are looking to purchse 3d printed items.

Dynamic elements

This site will make use of javascript moduels to allow for quick and easy access to the register of avalible items. Using this list, the site will dynamically update to show what is avalible. A search bar will also be provided.
